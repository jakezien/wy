Willka Yachay is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping indigenous communities survive in the modern world.

We empower native communities to guide themselves toward sustainable modernity, to elevate their standard of living, and to revitalize their cultural identity.

Who we help

Q’eros

Considered Peru’s last Incan community, the Q’eros Nation is a cultural treasure, yet its communities lack basics such as electricity, clean water, health care, and food security.

Learn more about the Q’eros Nation

What We Do

Projects

Willka Yachay provides Q’eros with diverse resources: we build and support schools, provide sustainable sources of energy and food, and nurture ancient culture.

See how we help

Our Schools

Schools

Willka Yachay builds schools high in the Andes. We collaborate with Q’eros not just on construction, but on developing the curriculum, school administration, and more.
