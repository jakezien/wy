Willka Yachay is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping indigenous communities thrive in the modern world.

We are empowering the next generation of the indigenous Q’eros Nation of Peru to become leaders who elevate their standard of living, guide their community toward sustainable modernity and revitalize their cultural identity.

Who We Help

Q’eros

{% include arrow.svg %}

Q’eros

Considered Peru’s last Incan community, the Q’eros people are a human treasure, yet they lack clean water, health care, food security, electricity and education.

What We Do

Projects

{% include arrow.svg %}

Projects

Together with the Q'eros, we build and sustain schools; implement solar programs, a weaving cooperative, & music and cultural preservation projects; provide food security, a medical center, & mother and infant care; and lead learning expeditions to Q’eros.

Our Education Initiative

Schools

{% include arrow.svg %}

Schools

Willka Yachay builds schools high in the Andes. We collaborate with Q’ero parents and elders on construction, administration and curriculum development. We employ culturally sensitive teachers and provide all supplies, nutritious food and educational (and fun) national and international field trips.
