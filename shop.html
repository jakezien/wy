Shop

Willka Yachay and Q’eros families have formed a cooperative to share their traditional weavings at a just price. Our collaboration celebrates an ancient and beautiful tradition at the heart of Q’eros culture, promotes its perpetuation by younger generations and enhances family and community economic security.

"What was earlier invisible to me became evident. The presence of mountain spirits was everywhere, even in the devotional weavings of the Indians. Their ancient textile techniques connected past to present: a continuous thread passed from generation to generation over millenia."
